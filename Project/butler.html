Butler University

Butler, a.k.a. "the Bulldogs," is located in Indianapolis, IN, and has an enrollment size of 4,200 students. On March 20, 2013, the school's administration announced that Butler would be joining the Big East Conference on July 1, 2013.

Roster

Championships

Butler has never won the NCAA Tournament, but they have made it to the finals twice, losing to Duke in 2010 and UCONN in 2011. They have qualified for the tournament 5 times since joining the Big East Conference, but have only advanced to the Sweet Sixteen one time (2017).
