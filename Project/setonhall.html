Seton Hall University

Seton Hall, a.k.a. "the Pirates," is located in South Orange, NJ, and has an enrollment size of 9,700 students. Seton Hall joined the Big East Conference in 1979 and decided to stay after the conference realignment in 2013.

Championships

Seton Hall University has appeared in the NCAA Tournament a total of 13 times. They have only advanced to the Elite Eight twice (1989, 1991) and has advanced to the Final Four and Championship rounds once (1989), losing to #3 Michicgan in OT 79-80. They are also three time Confernce Tournament Winners (1991, 1993, 2016).
