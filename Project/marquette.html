Marquette University

Marquette University is a private Jesuit university located in Milwaukee, WI. Founded as Marquette College in 1881, the school has an enrollment size of 11,782 students. Marquette joined the Big East in 2005 and persisted as a member through the 2013 realignment.

Championships

Marquette has not won an NCAA Tournament since joining the Big East in 2005. However, in the same span, the Golden Eagles have consistently made the Tournament playing field, only missing five tournaments (2005, 2014, 2015, 2016, 2018).
