Providence College

Providence, a.k.a. "the Friars," is located in Providence, RI, and has an enrollment size of 3,866 students. Providence joined the original Big East Conference in 1979 and decided to stay after the conference realignment in 2013.

Championships

Since joining the Big East Conference, Providence College has appeared in the NCAA Tournament a total of 12 times. The furthest they have advanced is the Final Four (1987). The Friars are also two time Conference Tournament Champions, having won in 1994 and 2014.
