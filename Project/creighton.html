Creighton University

Creighton, a.k.a. "the Bluejays," is located in Omaha, NE, and has an enrollment size of 8,236 students. Creighton joined the Big East Conference following the conference realignment in 2013.

Championships

Creighton has never advanced past the Elite Eight in the NCAA Tournament, and has only advanced that far one time (1941). Since joining the Big East Conference, Creighton has qualified for the tournament a total of four times (2013, 2014, 2017, and 2018). They were conference regular season champs in 2013 and 2020, and won the conference tournament in 2013.
