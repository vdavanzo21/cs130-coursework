Villanova

Villanova is a private university in Villanova, PA, with an enrollment of 6,400. The school joined the Big East Conference in 2013.

Roster

Championships

Villanova has won four Big East championships since 2013 (2015, 2017, 2018, 2019), and won the 2016 and 2018 NCAA Men's Basketball Championships over the same span. Historically, the Wildcats have won three additional conference championships, as well as another NCAA championship in 1985.
