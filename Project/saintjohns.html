St. John's University

St. John's University, a.k.a. "the Red Storm," was founded in 1870 in Queens, NY, and has an enrollment size of 19,047 students. St. John's joined the Big East Conference in 1979.

Championships

The St. John's men's basketball team played it's first game on December 6th, 1907. While they have never won the NCAA Men's Tournament, they are four time Big East Conference Tournament Champions, having won in 1983, 1986, 2000, and 2019-20.
