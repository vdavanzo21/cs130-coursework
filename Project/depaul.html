DePaul University

DePaul is a private, Roman Catholic university located in Chicago, IL. Founded in 1898, DePaul has an enrollment size of just under 25,000. The Blue Demons joined the Big East in 2005 and stayed when the conference was realigned in 2013.

Championships

The Blue Demons have not made the NCAA Tournament as a member of the Big East conference. Prior to 2005, the team had made two separate Final Four appearances, in 1943 and 1979.
