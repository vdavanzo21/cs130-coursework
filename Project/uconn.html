University of Connecticut (UConn)

The University of Connecticut is a public university whose flagship campus is located in Storrs, CT. The school has an enrollment size of around 32,000 students and is expected to rejoin the Big East conference for the next college basketball season in 2020 or beyond.

Championships

As a member of the Big East prior to conference realignment from 1979-2013, the Huskies won 19 Big East conference championships, as well as three NCAA Tournaments (1999, 2004, 2011). UConn won a fourth NCAA Tournament in 2014 as a member of the American Conference.
