Xavier University

Xavier University is located in Cincinnati, OH. Founded in 1831, Xavier is the fourth-oldest Jesuit university in the United States. The school has an enrollment total of 4,485 students and joined the Big East in 2013.

Championships

While they have not won an NCAA Tournament or Big East title, the Musketeers have still found success, making the Elite Eight in 2017. Of all NCAA teams that have never made a Final Four, Xavier currently has the most NCAA Tournament wins with 27.
