Georgetown University

Georgetown, a.k.a "the Hoyas," is located in Washington, D.C. The school was founded in 1789 and has an enrollment size of 6,806 students. Georgetown joined the Big East Conference on July 1st, 2013.

Championships

Since joining the Big East in 2013, the Hoyas have yet to see much success in the NCAA Men's Basketball Tournament. They have appeared in the tournament thirty times, but have not won a Championship since 1984. Despite their lack of success in the NCAA tournament, the Hoyas have won the Big East Conference tournament a record seven times.
